Milestone School

Milestone Kinder Garden & School is one of the renowned kinder Garden and school in Dhangadhi. It is located at BHanuchowk, Dhangadhi, 500 meters from Bhanu chowk. It lies at a peaceful location away which is away from the traffic and city crowd, which heps children to broaden the mental horizon in open and fresh surroundings.


Milestone Kinder Garden & School is committed to academic excellence. The school is dedicated to maintain the academic environment with the help of outstanding faculty engaged in the task of creative sensibility, or sense of responsibility and moral integrity.
